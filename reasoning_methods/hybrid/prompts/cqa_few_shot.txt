Q: What organ in the human body is primarily responsible for pumping blood?
Answer Choices:
(a) liver
(b) brain
(c) heart
(d) kidney
(e) lung
A: The organ responsible for pumping blood throughout the body is the heart. It functions as a muscular pump that circulates blood, delivering oxygen and nutrients. The other options serve different roles: the liver processes toxins, the brain controls bodily functions, the kidneys filter blood, and the lungs are responsible for gas exchange.
Therefore, the answer is heart (c).

Q: What is the chemical symbol for water?
Answer Choices:
(a) CO₂
(b) NaCl
(c) H₂O
(d) O₂
(e) CH₄
A: Water is composed of two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom. The chemical formula for this composition is H₂O. CO₂ is carbon dioxide, NaCl is salt, O₂ is oxygen gas, and CH₄ is methane.
Therefore, the answer is H₂O (c).

Q: Which of these shapes has three sides?
Answer Choices:
(a) square
(b) rectangle
(c) triangle
(d) circle
(e) pentagon
A: A triangle is a polygon with exactly three sides. A square and rectangle each have four sides, a circle has no sides, and a pentagon has five sides.
Therefore, the answer is triangle (c).

Q: Which planet is known as the Red Planet?
Answer Choices:
(a) Venus
(b) Earth
(c) Jupiter
(d) Mars
(e) Saturn
A: Mars is known as the Red Planet due to its reddish appearance, which comes from iron oxide (rust) on its surface. The other planets do not have this characteristic.

Q: In programming, what does HTML primarily define?
Answer Choices:
(a) the logic of the program
(b) the behavior of dynamic elements
(c) the structure of a webpage
(d) the styling of elements
(e) the backend database
A: HTML (HyperText Markup Language) is used to define the structure and content of webpages. It lays out headings, paragraphs, lists, links, etc. Styling is done with CSS, and logic with JavaScript.
Therefore, the answer is the structure of a webpage (c).

Q: What is the primary gas found in the Earth's atmosphere?
Answer Choices:
(a) oxygen
(b) carbon dioxide
(c) helium
(d) nitrogen
(e) hydrogen
A: About 78% of Earth's atmosphere is composed of nitrogen. Oxygen makes up around 21%, and the rest includes trace gases like argon, CO₂, and others.
Therefore, the answer is nitrogen (d).

Q: Which of the following animals is a mammal?
Answer Choices:
(a) frog
(b) eagle
(c) dolphin
(d) snake
(e) salmon
A: Dolphins are warm-blooded, give birth to live young, and nurse them with milk—all characteristics of mammals. Frogs are amphibians, eagles are birds, snakes are reptiles, and salmon are fish.
Therefore, the answer is dolphin (c).

Q: What historical event began in 1939 and involved many nations?
Answer Choices:
(a) World War I
(b) The Cold War
(c) World War II
(d) The Industrial Revolution
(e) The Great Depression
A: World War II began in 1939 with the invasion of Poland by Germany, leading to a global conflict involving many countries. World War I began in 1914, the Cold War started post-1945, the Industrial Revolution began in the 18th century, and the Great Depression started in 1929.
Therefore, the answer is World War II (c).

Q: Which part of the computer is considered the "brain"?
Answer Choices:
(a) hard drive
(b) RAM
(c) motherboard
(d) CPU
(e) monitor
A: The CPU (Central Processing Unit) performs most of the processing inside a computer and is often referred to as its "brain." The other components support different functions: storage, memory, communication, or display.
